Essential Tips

🌱 Care Fundamentals

  • Check soil moisture before watering — stick your finger 2 cm deep.
  • Most houseplants prefer indirect bright light, not direct harsh sun.
  • Yellowing leaves = overwatering; brown tips = low humidity or underwatering.
  • Feed with diluted fertiliser every 2–4 weeks during the growing season.
  • Repot when roots circle the drainage hole — go one pot size up only.
  • Wipe dusty leaves with a damp cloth to improve photosynthesis.
